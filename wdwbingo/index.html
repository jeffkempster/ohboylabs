WDW Bingo

Disney World Scavenger Hunt

WDW Bingo brings a whole new level of adventure to your Disney World experience! Designed to play right from your iPhone, WDW Bingo is perfect for families, friends, and Disney fans of all ages who want to make the most of their time while waiting in line or strolling through the park.

Play everywhere

Whether you're exploring the Magic Kingdom, EPCOT, Hollywood Studios, Animal Kingdom, Disney Springs, or even the Disney Resorts, this fun scavenger hunt game lets you discover hidden details and beloved icons all around the parks. Start hunting, spotting, and marking off your Disney finds to win—no two games are ever the same!

All the favorites

WDW Bingo makes the wait part of the fun! With 42 unique games available throughout the Walt Disney World Resort, our scavenger hunt adventure turns time spent in queue lines into a game of discovery.

From Big Thunder Mountain and TTA PeopleMover to Haunted Mansion, Figment, Remy’s Ratatouille Adventure, and Avatar Flight of Passage, every queue line has its own exciting details to spot. Instead of just waiting, you can dive into a world of hidden Disney magic with your family or friends, checking off Disney details, secrets, and iconic elements unique to each ride’s atmosphere.

Bingo!

Each game presents a 5x5 bingo card, complete with a free space in the center, and challenges you to mark five in a row—across, down, or diagonal—to win. Every card is randomized with unique items to find in the queue line, making each game fresh and exciting. Play alongside others and see who can score bingo first!

Achievement Coins

As you play WDW Bingo, your discoveries don’t go unnoticed! The game tracks your progress and awards special Achievement coins when you reach certain milestones, adding an extra layer of excitement to your adventure. From uncovering details in EPCOT's World Showcase to Tomorrowland, Batuu, and even Disney restaurants, these achievements celebrate your keen eye and Disney expertise. Collecting coins for each accomplishment gives you goals to strive for and lets you showcase your WDW Bingo mastery!
