Learning Decks

Learning Decks is a great tool to help you and your child learn together. It is a digital flash card tool with controls simple enough for you child to use alone, but also packed with features to help further their education.

Made for Kids and Kid Tested

Your kids will learn topics like: Shapes, Letters, Numbers, Animals, Space, Transportation and more. New decks and cards will further enhance your experience.

Features
  • Stats - Easily see how often cards are viewed. A total and a views by day will give great insight into the learning.
  • Speak - Tapping on each card will speak the card's title.
  • Random Deck - This feature will randomly choose cards from all decks to really mix up the learning.
  • Hide/Show Title - Easily hide or show the title of the card.
  • Shuffle the Deck - Each deck can be presented in order or shuffled.
